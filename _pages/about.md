I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the [University of Wisconsin-Madison](https://www.wisc.edu/), advised by [Ming Liu](https://pages.cs.wisc.edu/~mgliu/). I received my B.S. in Computer Science from the [University of Toronto](https://www.utoronto.ca/) (St. George Campus), where I was advised by [Qizhen Zhang](https://qizhenzhang.me/) and [Phil Bernstein](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/philbe/).

My research interests lie in **MLSys**, **efficient ML**, and **GPU communication**. I have contributed to [UCCL-EP](https://github.com/uccl-project/uccl) ![GitHub stars](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/uccl-project/uccl?style=social) (portable expert-parallel communication for LLMs), and [Sirius](https://github.com/sirius-db/sirius) ![GitHub stars](https://img.shields.io/github/stars/sirius-db/sirius?style=social) (GPU-accelerated SQL engine). I am always open to new ideas and collaborations -- feel free to reach out!
