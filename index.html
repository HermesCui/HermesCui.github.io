I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, advised by Ming Liu. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Toronto (St. George Campus), where I was advised by Qizhen Zhang and Phil Bernstein.

My research interests lie in MLSys, efficient ML, and GPU communication. I have contributed to UCCL-EP (portable expert-parallel communication for LLMs), and Sirius (GPU-accelerated SQL engine). I am always open to new ideas and collaborations – feel free to reach out!
